Chapter 4 - Network Access

Fiber-Optic Cabling

Advantages

Advantages of Fiber-Optic Cabling are:

  • long distance traveling
  • high bandwidth
  • signal with less attenuation
  • completely immune to EMI, RFI

Optical Fiber

Optical Fiber is:

  • extremely thin
  • transparent pure glass

Bits in optical fiber are transfered as light impulses

Fiber-Optic Cable

This cable transmit data in the light - there is minumum loss of signal.
It looks like WC roll with the mirror inside of it and small laser send Morse code to it with speed of light.

Fiber vs Copper

Implementation Issues UTP Cabling Fiber-optic Cabling
Bandwidth supported 10 Mb/s - 10 Gb/s 10 Mb/s - 100 Gb/s
